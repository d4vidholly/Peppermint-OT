NDIS Occupational Therapy Services


PeppermintOT offers a range of services designed to meet the needs of children and young people. The following Assessments are available for Plan Managed and Self Managed NDIS participants only. NDIS OT services are $193.99 per hour (or per price guide). Travel will be charged as per NDIS price guide.

  • Functional Capacity Assessments (FCA) evaluate abilities in daily life, providing a detailed report to outline necessary support.
  • Home and Living Assessments (SDA/SIL/ILO) evaluate housing needs, daily tasks, and support, to identify modifications for a safe, accessible environment.

We also offer Occupational Therapy Practice Supervision for New Graduates and/or Overseas Qualified OTs seeking full AHPRA registration. Please get in touch for more information.
